    How to write a better search snippet

    A strong search snippet tells people what the page is about, why it is useful, and why it is worth opening now. Start with the search intent, place the primary topic near the beginning, and remove filler words that do not help the click decision.

    For most pages, a practical SEO title target is 45 to 60 characters. A practical meta description target is 120 to 155 characters. These are not ranking rules, but they keep your message easier to scan in search results.

    Quick checklist

    • Put the main topic in the title and description.
    • Use a concrete benefit instead of a vague promise.
    • Match the page content; do not bait the click.
    • Write one clear result preview before publishing.

    Recommended SEO workflow

    Use 1SO to tighten your snippet, then check the live page in Google Search Console after indexing. When your page gets impressions but low clicks, test a clearer title first.

    FAQ

    Is 1SO free?

    Yes. The SERP snippet preview and title ideas are free to use.

    Can a better meta description improve SEO?

    A meta description is not a direct ranking shortcut, but a clearer snippet can help the right searchers choose your result.

    Does Google always show my title and description?

    No. Google may rewrite snippets based on the query and page content. Your title and description still help communicate the intended result.

    What is GEO optimization?

    Generative engine optimization means making your page easy for AI search systems to understand, quote, and summarize through clear definitions, structured answers, and trustworthy page context.
