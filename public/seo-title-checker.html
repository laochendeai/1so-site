What is an SEO title checker?

An SEO title checker is a tool that reviews a page title for clarity, length, keyword use, and search intent match before publication.

What makes a good SEO title?

A good SEO title describes the page with enough detail for a searcher to decide whether the result is relevant. It should match the search intent, include the main topic naturally, and avoid generic phrases that could fit any page.

For most pages, a practical title length target is 45 to 60 characters. This is not a fixed Google rule. It is a useful editing range because it forces the title to be specific while reducing the chance of visible truncation.

SEO title checklist

  • Place the main topic near the beginning when it reads naturally.
  • Use one clear benefit or page type, such as tool, guide, checklist, template, or calculator.
  • Remove repeated brand names, filler adjectives, and empty claims.
  • Make the title honest. A misleading title may win a click but lose trust quickly.
