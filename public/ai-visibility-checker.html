What does the AI Visibility Checker measure?

The AI Visibility Checker tests whether a public page has crawlable metadata, structured headings, canonical tags, schema, an answer-ready section, AI crawler access, llms.txt, and sitemap coverage. These signals help AI search systems understand and retrieve a page, but they do not guarantee citations or traffic.
